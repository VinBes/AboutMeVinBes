Born in the Netherlands, based in Hong Kong, Vence is always looking to share new experiences and love for music. Passionately exploring sounds across the electronic spectrum feeling into what is good.

Vence started playing music in local The Hague venues, and soon made his way to the rooftop parties of Barcelona. As he moved further south, he found a second home in Tunisian clubs and festivals.

After moving to Hong Kong he joined the Sol Passion agency and is steadily gaining traction in the city's vibrant electronic music scene.
